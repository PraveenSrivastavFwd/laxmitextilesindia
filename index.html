ABOUT US

  • Welcome to Laxmi Textiles, your one-stop destination for authentic and high-quality sarees! We are a family-owned business that has been operating for over 50 years, bringing the best of traditional and contemporary Indian sarees to customers worldwide.
  • At Laxmi Textiles, we believe that sarees are not just a piece of clothing but a reflection of Indian culture and tradition. That's why we take great pride in curating the finest and most exquisite sarees from all over India, ensuring that our customers receive only the best.
  • From the intricately woven Banarasi silk sarees to the delicate Chanderi cotton sarees, we have a vast collection that caters to every taste and occasion.
  • Our team consists of passionate individuals who have a deep understanding and love for sarees. They work tirelessly to ensure that each saree is carefully selected, inspected, and curated before it reaches our customers. With our extensive knowledge and experience in the industry, we can guarantee that every saree you purchase from us is of the highest quality.
  • We understand that sarees hold a special place in the hearts of many, and that's why we strive to provide an unforgettable shopping experience to all our customers. We offer a hassle-free online shopping experience, with easy payment options and quick delivery services.
  • We also have a dedicated customer service team that is always ready to assist you with any queries or concerns you may have.
  • At Laxmi Textiles, we are committed to preserving the art and heritage of Indian sarees. We work with weavers and artisans from all over India, providing them with a platform to showcase their talent and earn a sustainable livelihood. By supporting us, you are not just purchasing a saree but contributing to the betterment of our community.
  • We take great pride in being a part of your special moments, be it weddings, festivals, or any other occasion. We hope to continue being a part of your journey and providing you with the best of Indian sarees.
  • Thank you for choosing Laxmi Textiles!❤
